## 📑 Understanding Mining Terms

- **Drops:** Digital items granted for watching participating streams.
- **Campaign:** A specific timeframe where rewards become available.
- **Farmer:** The process of automating your presence in a channel to generate progress toward a goal.
- **Miner:** The internal script that manages your watch time metrics.
- **Raids:** Incoming viewers from other channels that might impact the software's refresh rate. We handle these automatically.
